Usability Test Results

About

In this usability assessment, we delve into the user experience of an automotive-related website. Our tester was told to complete five specific tasks, ranging from finding affordable automotive antifreeze for RVs to locating the nearest auto-repair shop using their location services. Each task came with its own set of challenges, observations, and time assessments, providing valuable insights into the website's functionality and user-friendliness.

Task: Find the cheapest auto-motive antiefreeze for RVs

  1. Searches for RVs
  2. Looks for price range
  3. Sorted by price range

Time Elasped: 69 seconds

Task: Find the closest auto-repair shop using our location

  1. Seems confused at first
  2. Goes to location selector
  3. Website tracked location - automatically joined

Time Elasped: 22 seconds

Task: Find step by step instructions on how to replace an accessory drive belt

  1. Goes to know how
  2. Goes replacing a belt

Time Elasped: 24 seconds

Task: Find sun shades and place them in your shopping cart

  1. Goes to search bar
  2. Searches up sun shades

Time Elasped: 15 seconds

Task: Find the email and phone number to contact the company

  1. Scrolled all the way down (too many things on the bottom of page)
  2. Thinks it's somewhere in “about NAPA”
  3. Goes to sidebar
  4. Finds "Contact us

Time Elasped: 37 seconds

SUS Score

On a scale of 1-5 where 1 is strongly disagree and 5 is strongly agree.

Conclusion

In conclusion, this usability assessment highlights that the website's usability is generally above average. The user's feedback indicates that while the site is reasonably user-friendly and intuitive, they might not actively seek it out. They pointed out several distractions on the website but acknowledged that most elements were generally easy to find. The website's layout and navigation were described as intuitive, contributing to its overall usability. However, the user also suggested that addressing a few minor adjustments could significantly enhance the user experience. In summary, while the website performs well, addressing these distractions and fine-tuning user interactions could elevate it to an exceptional level of usability.
